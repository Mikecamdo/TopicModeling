Owls in the XFL - Week 7
4.3.23 | FOOTBALL


PHILADELPHIA - Week 7 of the XFL provided our former Owls with another opportunity to put their skills on display. 

On Friday night, Isaiah Graham-Mobley (2017-20) and the Arlington Renegades lost to the Seattle Sea Dragons, 24-15. Graham-Mobley recorded one tackle.

On Saturday night, Ryquell Armstead (2015-18) and the D.C. Defenders lost for just the first time this season, at the hands of the Orlando Guardians, 37-36. Armstead had seven carries for 25 yards, averaging 3.6 yards per rush.
